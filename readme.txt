== Description ==

Do you spend hours of you time to remove spam groups in BuddyPress?
With this plugin you can get rid of them quickly and easy!

Just check the checkboxes on the "Multi Group Remove" page under the BuddyPress menu and click the "Remove selected groups" button and you are done!

It is a very simple but effective plugin, it does not have any other management options for groups.
